Oscar's Bistro is a spacious cafe on ecclesall road. It serves cusomers coffee and tea 
from [Pollards](https://pollardscoffee.co.uk/), Yorkshire's oldest Coffee Roaster and a 
fan favourite for people in the local area. Oscar's is run by Martin and Lewis, who are 
excited to bring their experience with coffee and food to eccy road for us to enjoy.

As well as food and drinks, Oscar's bistro regularly hosts music evenings. These unplugged 
acoustic sets are a fantastic opportunity to meet those in the local area and have a sing-along 
in a safe and relaxed environment. You'll spot announcements for these events on their 
[Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/oscars_s11/), so be sure to give them a follow!
